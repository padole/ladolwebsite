Our Services In LADOL

Logistics and Construction Equipment

LADOL has logistics equipment already certified and in use. This includes:

Cranes, Forklifts,Trucks, Flatbed trailer(s), Tug(s), Flat barges, Fuel barges, Work barges, Ferries and speed boats, Earth moving equipment.

Rigging and Ship Repair

Project planning and supervision, Steel replacement on a per-ton basis, Blasting and painting services, NDT Testing and International Certification, Scaffolding services, Marine valve services and installation minimal transport costs, Procurement service for certified rolled steel and welding consumables, General workshop services Round-the-clock personnel support, accommodation and catering for local and international staff Long-term storage and inventory management, ensuring optimal efficiency and minimal waste.

Berthing and Vessel Handling

LADOL is registered as an ISPS-certified port facility with IMO, and receives local and international vessels. Featuring a 200m quay, 8.5m draft, 25-ton/m2 high load bearing area and additional 30-ton bollards at either end, LADOL’s quay can accommodate up to six supply vessels and three Heavy Lift Vessels simultaneously. The berthing crew works 24 hours a day and, since products are piped directly through ducts in the quay, vessel turnaround time can be less than 8 hours.

Sea Fastening and Welding

Fully equipped, certified welding crews are provided as part of LADOL’s logistics services. These crews work around the clock to ensure that projects are completed on schedule.

Large Fabrication Works

The Free Zone currently has several hectares of developed and serviced land that is ideal for large fabrication, from pipe coating to jacket and FPSO topside manufacture/assembly.

Sea Fastening and Welding

Fully equipped, certified welding crews are provided as part of LADOL’s logistics services. These crews work around the clock to ensure that projects are completed on schedule.

Hotel Accommodation

A modern, secure and comfortable 100-room hotel with recreational facilities is located within walking distance of the operational area.

Bulk and Mud Services

LADOL has prepared extensive laydown areas suitable for the construction of bulk and mud plants.

General infrastructure and Security

LADOL Free Zone has round-the clock power, water, Internet access, and all the other conveniences of modern life. The Base uses the latest infrastructure, IT and personnel security solutions. All the operational and personnel areas are fenced and can only be accessed through secure access control offices. Armed and unarmed guards patrol the area 24hours a day and IT solutions are deployed throughout, including CCTV.

Water and Fuel

Water is piped directly to the quay and the base has an extensive water treatment plant with over 1,000,000 liters of storage and two boreholes. Fuel is provided by LADOL fuel barges and through bulk fuel facilities that are currently under construction.

Personnel Transport into LADOL Free Zone

LADOL operates scheduled passenger transport for Free Zone staff and workers. This ferry service runs on a set timetable, several times a day, from secure locations on the mainland. The modern and comfortable passenger boats are exclusively for LADOL staff and workers to go to and from the Zone.

Certification

LADOL subcontractors work on- and offshore to provide equipment and personnel certification.

Why Choose LADOL

LADOL is Fully Integrated

LADOL is a fully integrated, independent and secure base anchored around a 200m quay with a deep, 8.5m draft. The QUAY provides stevedoring and cargo handling with efficient discharge and loading of goods. The BASE provides all logistical services, including warehousing, material handling equipment, fabrication and assembly yards, people management, catering, bunkering of fuel and lots more.

A High Value Industrial Free Zone

LADOL Free Zone was built out of a disused swamp on a secure island inside Apapa Port. This new, state-of-the-art Free Zone is strategically located at the point of entry into Lagos Harbour and it provides fully serviced facilities and heavy infrastructure designed and deployed to support the largest industrial projects in the world.

Purpose build facilities

LADOL is the first and only base specifically designed to meet the logistical needs of deep water offshore oil and gas operations and projects in and around Nigeria. As the first and only facility of its kind in Lagos, LADOL was created to ensure that the nation of Nigeria benefits directly.

Ideal location in Lagos harbour

The location of the Base and the services it offers will enable operator and contractors to maximize their operational efficiency. Manufacturers setting up on the base will find LADOL has excellent infrastructure.
