Our Services In LADOL

Ports Services & Cargo Handling

LADOL is the first and only base specifically designed to meet the logistical needs of deep water offshore oil and gas operations and projects in and around Nigeria. As the first and only facility of its kind in Lagos, LADOL was created to ensure that the nation of Nigeria benefits directly from its deep offshore oil and gas reserves.

Vessel Handling at Quay wall

LADOL is registered as an ISPS-certified port facility with IMO, and receives local and international vessels. Featuring a 200m quay, 8.5m draft, 25-ton/m2 high load bearing area and additional 30-ton bollards at either end, LADOL’s quay can accommodate up to six supply vessels and three Heavy Lift Vessels simultaneously.

Special Materials Handling

The BASE provides all logistical services, including warehousing, material handling equipment, fabrication and assembly yards, people management, catering, bunkering of fuel and water, facilities for the supply of bulk materials, a helicopter base, open and closed storage facilities, sewage and waste treatment, potable water and medical services.

Construction & Finishing

Fully equipped, certified welding crews are provided as part of LADOL’s logistics services. These crews work around the clock to ensure that projects are completed on schedule.

Positioned strategically at the entry point to Lagos harbor, LADOL provides a one-stop-shop for multinational industrial and oil and gas companies operating in West Africa.
